About Me

I started WDSA (Westhoff Design Support Automation) over years ago to do what I enjoy most: helping machines work smarter. As an all-round system engineer for off-road applications, I bring together mechanics, hydraulics, electronics, and software to create practical solutions that actually work in the field.

Over the years I’ve worked with:

  • Machine automation and mechatronics
  • Hydraulic and hydrostatic systems
  • Control systems (C, C++, Codesys, SiemensLogo, DCL Realizer)
  • Communication (CAN-bus, Serial-bus, custom gateways)
  • GPS positioning and autopilot systems
  • Data acquisition, flight recording, and troubleshooting

My specialties include GPS-ploughing, autopilot system integration, communication gateways, and black-box troubleshooting. Whether the issue is mechanical, hydraulic, electrical, or software, I take pride in analyzing, pinpointing, and solving problems — or finding a smart workaround if needed.

If you’re looking for a flexible partner who can jump in, understand your system, and help you move forward, I’d be glad to support your project.
